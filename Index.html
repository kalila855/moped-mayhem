Aid to Cambodia

Game Instructions: W for up, A for left, D for right. To complete each level, collect as many medicine bags as possible and reach the hospital.

Like many third world countries, Cambodia lacks accessible and affordable healthcare. Cambodia experienced a horrible genocide and communist tyranny from 1975 to 1979 that took the lives of about 1/4 of the population and almost all of the educated people. Cambodia is still struggling to recover from that tragedy; there are not enough good doctors or hospitals.

One of our team members traveled to Cambodia this year on a medical missions trip. If you would like to learn more please click 'More Info' below.
