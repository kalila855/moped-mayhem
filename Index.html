Trouble in Cambodia

Game Instructions: Use arrow keys to move. To complete each level, collect as many medicine bags as possible and reach the hospital.

Like our game? A little bit of background: Cambodia, a Southeastern Asian country, lacks accessible and affordable healthcare. Cambodia experienced a horrible genocide and communist tyranny from 1975 to 1979 that took the lives of about 1/4 of the population and almost all of the educated people. Cambodia is still struggling to recover from that tragedy; there are not enough professional doctors or hospitals.

One of our team members traveled to Cambodia this year on a medical missions trip. If you would like to learn more please click 'More Info' below.
